DRUGS SYMPTOMS ADVICE
Alcohol Drowsiness, insomnia, headaches, and trouble sleeping. Limit alcohol intake, avoid driving, and stay hydrated.

Heroin Severe headaches, nausea, vomiting, and dizziness. Limit heroin intake, avoid driving, and stay hydrated.
Marijuana Severe headaches, nausea, vomiting, and dizziness. Limit marijuana intake, avoid driving, and stay hydrated.
